Welcome to the Digital Archive of Robert F. Skitch

This website preserves the original autobiographical writings of my father, Robert Francis "Bob" Skitch, complemented by a rich gallery of original photographs and sketches.

I have published this collection to share his legacy - a deeply personal journey from a boy growing up in Western Australia to a Lieutenant Colonel commanding the Army Survey Regiment.

His memoirs serve as both a detailed family history and a unique window into the evolution of Australian military mapping and post-war society

The collection is divided into chronological chapters that chart the course of his life and career

Explore the Memoirs Interactively with NotebookLM

To make exploring my father's extensive memoirs as engaging and accessible as possible, I have uploaded the entire collection into a public Google NotebookLM project.
